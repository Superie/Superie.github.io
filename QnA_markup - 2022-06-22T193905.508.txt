				A: What is Fixed Pricing?
					Q(1.3.2.2.1.1): The fixed price plan locks the customer's rate in for a period of 1 year, starting on the 1st of the month and renews automatically each season. <br> A customer can enroll anytime of the year. <br><br> Please make sure the customer qualifies for the program by viewing the consumption.  <br><br> CER's are only able to enroll residential (LOB90) and small commercial (LOB 40).  Any other LOB should be referred to the MM for review <br><br> Here is a link to the Wiki for more information <a href="https://superiorplus.sharepoint.com/sites/KM/Wiki/Pages/Fixed%20Price%20Plan%20-%20New%20Enrollments.aspx#title2" target="_blank">Fixed Price Plans</a>
				A: What are the benefits?
					Q(1.3.2.2.1.2): <ul>
  <li>Great program for customers who like to have stability on their price per liter all year</li>
  <li>Peace of mind - If propane prices rise, they don't have to worry</li>
  <li>Renewal is automatic</li>
  <li>Can help to effectively budget year round heating costs</li>
  <li>Immune to unstable market prices</li>
  <li>Great retention solution when customers are concerned about their PPL.​</li>
</ul>  

				A: Eligibility
					Q(1.3.2.2.1.3): <ul>
  <li>Must be a residential or small commercial home heat customer **Note** Buying Groups​ (Association) customers are not eligible to enroll individually as t​heir member representatives work with our Account Managers directly to discuss rates. Refer to the <a href="https://superiorplus.sharepoint.com/sites/KM/Wiki/Pages/Buying%20Group%20Associations.aspx" target="_blank">Buying Groups/Associations Wiki page</a> for further details (</li>
  <li>Need to consume a total of 500L+ in the previous calendar year (or be forecasted to use this volume in the upcoming year.)</li>
  <li>Verbal Verification Agreement​​ process must be completed.</li>
  <li>Account must be in a good standing (​Failure to remain in good standing runs the risk of being de-enrolled and a de-enrollment fee being applied to the account)</li>
  <li>A tenant can be enrolled in FPP, as long as they are authorized on the account</li>
  <li>Customers who reside in regulated markets may not be eligible. See the National Pricing Tool for provinces affected</li>
  <li><a href="https://superiorplus.sharepoint.com/sites/KM/Wiki/Pages/Fixed%20Price%20Plan%20-%20New%20Enrollments.aspx#title4" target="_blank">Read more about it here</a>
