  • Great program for customers who like to have stability on their price per liter all year
  • Peace of mind - If propane prices rise, they don't have to worry
  • Renewal is automatic
  • Can help to effectively budget year round heating costs
  • Immune to unstable market prices
  • Great retention solution when customers are concerned about their PPL.
